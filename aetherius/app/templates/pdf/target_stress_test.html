{# ==================== METHODOLOGY & DISCLAIMER ==================== #}
08 · Methodology & disclaimer

How this deck was built

Each in-window observation was scored by the production Aetherius pipeline: (a) conservative entity mapping with word-boundary regex and an acronym stoplist; (b) a deterministic 7-factor risk-scoring formula (source confidence, freshness, directness, cross-confirmation, watchlist priority, event severity, relationship strength); (c) an adverse-language gate over a fixed {{ metadata.adverse_terms_taxonomy_size }}-term downside taxonomy. A flag is "shippable" only when severity reaches elevated or high AND at least one adverse term is present in the source text.

No LLM narrative is used to decide severity. Every score in this deck is deterministic from the observation dict and the scoring code. Re-running the same corpus at the same commit produces bit-identical results.
